We at Yogesh Verma Classes provide a complete guidance to aspiring commerce students in their journey for professional courses such as CA/CS/CMA.

CA/CS/CMA are distance learning courses, as a result of which many students have to depend on private coaching institutes which though give them the subject knowledge but fail to provide individual attention to each student as a result of which almost 80% students fail in the these examinations.

What’s the Solution ?

  • Proper time-scheduling of each subject
  • Regular assessment of the student’s performance on overall basis
  • Time to time guidance on enhancing their skill sets
  • Sorting out the issues faced by students in their academics
  • Extra curricular activities to overcome the gap caused due to non regular college
